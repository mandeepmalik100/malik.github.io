Gopika Premsankar

I am an Academy of Finland postdoctoral researcher at University of Helsinki working with Prof. Sasu Tarkoma.

I graduated with a PhD in Computer Science from Aalto University in Finland under the supervision of Prof. Mario Di Francesco. I received my Master’s degree in Mobile Computing - Services and Security from Aalto University and my Bachelor’s degree from the National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal.

My research interests are in edge computing and wireless communications. In particular, I have worked on optimizing resource allocation, infrastructure placement, and wireless network parameters in large-scale networks and edge deployments. I model networked systems and design solutions that can be applied in practical, large-scale settings.
